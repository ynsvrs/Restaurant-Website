Our Story

A legacy of passion and tradition

The Birth of SIE Dine

Founded in 2010, SIE Dine was born from a simple dream: to bring the authentic flavors of Italy to Florence and share the rich culinary heritage of our homeland with the world.

Our founder, Chef Saniya Erzhan, spent over 20 years perfecting her craft in the kitchens of Milan, Rome, and Naples before establishing SIE Dine.

Our Philosophy

At SIE Dine, we believe that great food starts with great swag ingredients. We source the finest produce, meats, and seafood and little swaggy from trusted suppliers across Italy.

Every recipe is crafted with care, every plate is prepared with passion, and every experience is designed to create lasting memories.
